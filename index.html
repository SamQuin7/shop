Perks Shop

Power Perks

VC Mod

Gain control over voice channels with the ability to mute, deafen, and move members between channels. Keep the conversation flowing smoothly.

Mod

Unlock powerful moderation tools: jail or free members, delete messages, view server logs (including deleted messages), timeout users, and manage image permissions. Includes all VC Mod abilities.

Senior Mod

Step up as a Senior Mod with access to everything from Mod and VC Mod, plus the added authority to ban members and demote users. Serious power for serious responsibility.

Head Administrator

The highest level of control. All powers from every role—VC Mod, Mod, and Senior Mod—are yours. Nothing is off-limits.

Ability Perks

Media Perms

Share your world! This role lets you post images and videos, stream, and turn on your camera. Express yourself with no limits (within server rules).

Custom Role

Craft a role that’s uniquely you. Pick any color, any symbol, and any font. Let your imagination run wild—just keep it within the server rules.
